Surabaya Tourism Spots

Places in Surabaya that you must visit!

Kebun Binatang Surabaya

KBS was one time, the most complete zoo in Southeast Asia, in which there are more than 981 different animal species consisting of more than 2,806 animals.

Tugu Pahlawan

Tugu Pahlawan is a monument that has become a landmark of the city of Surabaya. The height of this monument is 41.15 meters and is shaped like an inverted phallus or nail.

Taman Hiburan Pantai Kenjeran

Kenjeran Beach Amusement Park presents a coastal tourist attraction in the north of the city of Surabaya, the beautiful charm of the beach with a view of the Suramadu Bridge background can be enjoyed from here.

Taman Bungkul

Bungkul Park is a city tourism park located in the center of Surabaya, precisely on Jalan Raya Darmo. The park stands on an area of 900 square meters.

Kebun Bibit Wonorejo

KWonorejo Nursery Garden is a green area in Surabaya, East Java. Even though it is in the middle of the city, this area still looks green with lush trees

Monumen Kapal Selam

Located in the city center, this monument is actually the KRI Pasopati 410 submarine, one of the Republic of Indonesia Navy's fleet made by the Soviet Union in 1952.

Jembatan Suramadu

The bridge that crosses the Madura Strait. With a length of 5,438 m, the Suramadu bridge is currently the longest bridge in Indonesia.

House of Sampoerna

Tobacco museum and Sampoerna headquarters that is located in Surabaya. The architectural style of the main building which was influenced by the Dutch colonial style was built in 1862 and is now a historical site.

Taman Suroboyo

Suroboyo Park is located in front of the Bulak Fish Center (SIB). The park occupies an area of 11,000 square meters. What is very striking here is the Surabaya icon, namely the 25m high Suro Boyo Statue.
