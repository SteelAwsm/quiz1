The Local foods of Surabaya

Delicious local foods of Surabaya That you definitely have to try!

Rujak Cingur

The word cingur means "mouth", this refers to the ingridient that is a cut from the mouth or snout of the cow and is mixed into the dish.

Pecel Semanggi

Made from clover leaves that are steamed and then enjoyed with a delicious spicy chili sauce.

Lontong Balap

Consists of rice cake, bean sprouts, fried tofu, lentho, fried onions, soy sauce, and chili sauce.

Sate Klopo

Beef and fat satay wrapped in coconut-processed spices and then grilled. the coconut layer is what makes it unique.

Sate Karak

Made from pieces of beef offal, covered with a secret spice and grilled, served with black glutinous rice, and sprinkled with coconut seasoning called sambal bubul.

Tahu Tek

Consists of half-cooked fried tofu served with rice cake, potato wedges, bean sprouts, cucumber and petis seasoning, complemented by a sprinkling of crackers.

soto Gubeng

This soup is made from chicken, beef, equipped with green onions, fried onions, lime, white rice, and chili sauce.

Lontong Kupang

Lontong (rice cake) is served with kupang(small clam) which is cooked into soup. Kupang soup seasoning usually consists of garlic, salt, sugar, and white pepper.

Sate Lisidu

Sate ayam lisidu's shape is flat. At first glance the shape is similar to intestine satay, because it is flat and elongated. However, the ingredients for making it are chicken meat.
